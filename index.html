About G.P. Nainital

Established in 1956, Government Polytechnic Nainital is one of Uttarakhand's premier technical institutions offering diplomas in engineering and technology disciplines.

Admission

Admissions are done through the Uttarakhand Joint Entrance Examination for Polytechnics (JEEP). Eligible candidates can apply online through the official website of UBTER.

Features
